Installing new courses

To install a new course, from the homepage, press on the menu button and go to 'Manage courses'. This will give you a list of the courses to select to download. you will need an active internet connection to get the list of courses and to install them.

Updating courses

OppiaMobile will automatically alert you when there are new updates to your courses available and ready to download. You can also manually check for updates by visiting the manage courses page.

Installing/downloading media and videos

When you download a new course, the course download will not include any media or video files. This is to ensure that the course is not too large to download easily, since the videos may be very large. Instead, the media/video files should either be downloaded or copied to your phone SD card separately. OppiaMobile will alert you if any required videos are missing, you can then select to download them. Due to their file size (and to avoid you using up too much of your mobile data connection) videos can only be downloaded when you are on a wifi connection. if you don't have a wifi connection, you can download the files directly to your PC, and then copy them to the SD card form there, to do this, follow the instructions from the 'Download via PC' button to guide you through this.

Top

Playing media and videos

When there are videos in your course, when you press to play on them, OppiaMobile will use your default or preferred video media player. If you receive a message that the video could not be played, it is likely that you haven't yet downloaded that video.

What do the points mean?

OppiaMobile awards you points for taking part in activities, watching videos and for your quiz scores. The more regularly you take the activities, attempt quizzes and play the videos the more points you will earn.
